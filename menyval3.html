Smoothie ingredients

  • Apple and banana smoothie

    • 2dl yogurt, 2dl apple juice, 1 banana

  • Banana smoothie

    • 2 bananas, 2 teaspoons of chocolate drink powder, 1dl of vanilla yogurt.

  • Orange and pineapple smoothie

    • 2dl crushed pineapple, 1dl milk, 1dl vanilla yogurt, 1 tablespoon orange juice.

  • Srawbwrry and white chocolate smoothie

    • 200g frozen srawbwrry, 50g white chocolate, 2 teaspoons of lemon juice, 3dl vanilla yogurt.

  • Rasberry smoothie

    • 1dl freezed Rasberrys, 1 tablespoon of sugar, 1dl milk.

  • Blueberry and banana smoothie

    • 2 banana, 1dl freezed blueberry, 1dl vanilla yogurt, 1dl orange juice.
