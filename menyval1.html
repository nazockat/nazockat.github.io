Cake ingredients

  • Mountain chocolate cake

    • 3 egg, 3dl powder sugar, 2 teaspoon vanilla suger, 1dl cocoa, 2dl flour, 1dl nuts, 2dl minimarshmallows, 100g dark chocolate, coconut flakes.

  • Cake pops with gingerbread cookies

    • 10 barbecue sticks, 25 gingerbread cookies, 1dl kvarg, 200g dark chocolate, 1dll powder sugar.

  • Red velvet cake

    • 2 egg, 3dl powder sugar, 3dl flour, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon cocoa, 1 teaspoon vanilla suger, 2dl milk, 2 tablespoon red food coloring, 1dl rapeseed oil.

  • Lemon bundt cake

    • 6dl flour, 1 teaspoon salt, 175g butter, 5dl powder sugar, 5 egg, 1 lemon, 1dl sour cream, 1 teaspoon bicarbonate.

  • Kladdkaka

    • 100g butter, 2 egg, 1dl flour, 3 tablespoon cocoa, 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar.
