Cupcake ingredients

  • Red velvet cupcake

    • 50g butter, 4 egg, 2 tablespoon of red food coloring, 5dl flour, 5 tablespoons of cocoa, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 2 tablespoon vanilla sugar, 3dl powdered sugar.

  • Vanilla cupcake

    • 150g butter, 4 egg, 5dl flour, 2dl milk, 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar, 2 teaspoon baking soda.

  • Chocolate cupcake

    • 4dl flour, 1,8dl sugar, 1dl cocao, 1 egg, 3dl milk, 1 egg, 100g dark chocolate, 4 teaspoon baking soda, 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar, 1dl margarine.

  • Chocolate and srawberry cupcake

    • 125g butter, 2 egg, 250g kvarg vanilla, 75g dark chocolate, 150g fresh strawberrys, 1dl powder sugar, 2dl baking soda, coconut flakes.

  • blueberry and raspberry cupcake

    • 50g butter, 3 egg, 1dl milk, 4dl flour,4dl fresh raspberrys and blueberrys, 2dl powder sugar, 2 teaspoon baking soda.
